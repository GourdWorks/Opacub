Stromatolites

Time period: 3400 Million years ago - Present



Taxonomy: varied



Diet: The Sun

Lifespan: 2000 - 3000 years

Sexuality: asexual (prokaryotic, binary fission)

Habitat: Saline locations, coasts

Size: visible, up to a metre tall, most ancient ones are around 10 cm wide and 3 cm tall



Other information: Ruled the earth for billions of years and were very common in the proterozoic.
